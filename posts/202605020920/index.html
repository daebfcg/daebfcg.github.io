Nut and First String Up

Finishing up the nut and putting on strings for the first time

2026-5-2 14:20

Nut

Some preliminary work was done on the nut earlier. I am using a dyed black bone nut from StewMac. (I have in the past cut my own from bovine dog-chew bone from the grocery store.)

First sanded to width.

Then the length.

Lastly, the slots are cut and it is shaped up and polished.

First Stringing

The strings are on for the first time! DAddario XL110 for now. These will come off and on a few times during setup and will be replaced with a nicer Pyramid set in the end.

First Impressions: Plays nice and is quite bright (as expected). I played it like this for a couple hours to let it settle and to get a sense of things. Chords sound especially good - very clear. Sound is balanced but definitely leans to the high-end side. Single notes seem fine but will need to be evaluated through pickups and amp to really know.
