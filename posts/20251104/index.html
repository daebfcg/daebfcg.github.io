Laminating the Neck

Three-Ply Cherry/Walnut/Cherry Neck glue-up

2025-11-4 13:52

I am hand-jointing everything with a #7 plane. Would I like to have a jointer? Yes.

The #7 plane is a little more work, but when properly honed and used it leaves the surface dead flat and very smooth. It should make for some very tight joints.

I use hot hide glue exclusively. More on this topic later.

The two screws are used to keep the pieces in place while gluing. They are strategically placed so the hole will be covered up by another part later.
