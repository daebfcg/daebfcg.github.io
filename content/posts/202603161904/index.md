For the fretboard edge binding I first select a scrap of walnut that will yield some nice quartersawn strips.

{{<imgresize binding_stock.jpg "800x800" "Walnut stock for the fretboard binding">}}

From that I slice off a strip around 3/16 thick and about 3/4 wide.  Both binding strips will be made together to keep their thickness equal.  The target final size of each strip is 3/8 high by 3/32 thick.

{{<imgresize binding_slice.jpg "800x800" "Slice of walnut for edge binding">}}

This slice is sanded down close to 3/32 thick.

{{<imgresize binding_thicknessing.jpg "800x800" "Thickness sanding the fretboard edge binding">}}

And then taken down close to the final thickness using a random orbit sander followed by hand sanding.

{{<imgresize binding_sanding.jpg "800x800" "Sanding the binding to the right thickness">}}

A few passes with the card scraper and the thickness is right.

{{<imgresize binding_sanding_closeup.jpg "800x800" "Sanding the binding 3/32 inch thick">}}

Now to cut into two 3/8 inch wide strips using an Exacto knife.

{{<imgresize binding_cutting.jpg "600x600" "Cutting the binding into two equal strips">}}
