## First Stringing

First time strings are on.  DAddario XL110 for the time being.  These will come off and on a few times and will be replaced with a nicer set in the end.

{{<imgresize strings_on.jpg "800x800" "Strings on">}}

**First Impressions:** Plays nice and is quite _bright_ (as expected).  I played it like this for a couple hours to let it settle and to get a sense of things.   Chords sound especially good - very clear.  Sound is balanced but definitely leans to the high end side.  Single notes seem fine but will need to be evaluated through pickups and amp to really know.
